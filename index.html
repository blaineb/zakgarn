Zak Garn

My name is Zak Garn - I’m a young drummer making a start in the fertile Bay Area music community. I started north of the Golden Gate playing drums for many of the rising names from Sonoma County including Charlie Davenport, Frankie Boots, David Luning, Midori and Ezra Boy and more.

Since then I have finished a music degree from UC Berkeley where I studied under the amazing pianist and composer Myra Melford while working intimately with drummer and composer Scott Amendola. I am currently located near the ocean in San Francisco’s Outer Sunset and continue to search out opportunities to collaborate and play! Here’s a brief snippet of music I have made so far:
